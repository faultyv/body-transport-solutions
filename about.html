About Body Transport Solutions

BODY TRANSPORT SOLUTIONS (PTY) LTD is a South African transport-only company focused on refrigerated human-remains transport and repatriation support. We support funeral parlours, mortuaries, hospitals and approved service providers that require reliable, respectful and professional transport capacity.

In plain language

We are not a funeral parlour. We are not a mortuary. We do not prepare, embalm, store, cremate, bury or arrange funerals. We transport — that's our whole job, and we do it properly.
