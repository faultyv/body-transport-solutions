Compliance & documentation

BODY TRANSPORT SOLUTIONS operates as a transport-only service provider. All transport is subject to applicable South African legal, municipal, health and documentation requirements. The company does not replace official documents issued by the Department of Home Affairs, SAPS, hospitals, mortuaries, funeral parlours, municipalities or the Department of Health.

Compliance themes

Transport-only operation Refrigerated vehicle Vehicle cleaning and disinfection Chain-of-custody records Collection and receiving documentation Environmental Health / municipal guidance R363 Management of Human Remains awareness Client-provided authorisations and permits where required

Documents usually required or requested

Collection instruction from authorised client
Death documentation
Burial order where already issued
SAPS transport certificate or movement authority where required
Release note from hospital / mortuary / funeral parlour
Receiving note signed by destination
ID or passport copy of deceased if available
Special handling declaration where needed
Cleaning and disinfection log
Incident report if abnormal event occurs
Please note

Requirements may vary depending on location, cause of death, disease risk, destination, inter-provincial movement, cross-border movement, and the authorised parties involved. Clients must confirm official requirements with the relevant authorities where needed.
