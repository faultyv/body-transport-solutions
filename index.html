Common questions

Are you a funeral parlour?

No. We are a transport-only company. We do not arrange funerals, store, prepare, embalm, cremate or bury. We move human remains between approved facilities — that's our whole job.

Can a family contact you directly?

Yes — we'll gladly take your call and guide you, but transport is carried out in support of an authorised funeral parlour, mortuary, hospital or insurer, with the correct documentation in place.

How quickly can you collect?

We operate 24/7, including after-hours and weekends. Once the paperwork and route are confirmed, we dispatch — availability depends on scheduling, distance and vehicle readiness.

Do you cover the whole of South Africa?

Yes. We are based in KwaZulu-Natal (Cato Ridge / Camperdown / Durban corridor) and run routes to all nine provinces.

What paperwork is needed?

Typically a collection instruction, death documentation, a release note from the facility and a receiving note at the destination. We confirm exactly what's needed for your route when you contact us — see our Compliance page.

Need urgent transport right now?

Call or WhatsApp — we confirm the collection point, destination, paperwork, availability and route in one conversation.
