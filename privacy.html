Privacy & cookies

Clear handling of website and enquiry data.

This notice explains how Body Transport Solutions (Pty) Ltd processes information submitted through this website and how optional advertising measurement works.

The short versionEssential website functions operate without advertising cookies. The Meta Pixel loads only after you choose “Allow measurement”. We do not send names, email addresses, phone numbers or transport-form contents to Meta.

Who is responsible

Body Transport Solutions (Pty) Ltd, registration number 2026/464849/07, is responsible for the information collected through this website.

Privacy enquiries may be sent to info@bodytransportsolutions.co.za or discussed on +27 61 589 1505.

Information we process

  • Contact and organisation details supplied in a transport enquiry.
  • Collection point, destination, requested timing, documentation status and operational instructions supplied for a proposed transport job.
  • Technical request information normally transmitted when a browser connects to a website, such as IP address, device and browser information.
  • With permission, website visits and contact actions measured through the Meta Pixel.

Please provide only the case information needed to assess and coordinate the transport request. Do not include unrelated medical or personal information.

Why we use it

Enquiry information is used to respond, assess route and vehicle availability, prepare a quote, coordinate an authorised collection and delivery, maintain required operational records, prevent misuse and meet applicable legal obligations.

Where applicable, processing is based on steps requested before entering into a service arrangement, performance of that arrangement, legitimate business and security interests, legal obligations, or consent for optional advertising measurement.

Service providers and transfers

The transport request form is delivered using FormSubmit. Hosting and content delivery are provided by Vercel. Optional advertising measurement is provided by Meta Platforms. These providers may process technical information in countries outside South Africa. Their own safeguards and privacy terms also apply.

We do not sell personal information. We disclose it only where needed to operate the website, respond to an enquiry, deliver an authorised service, protect rights and safety, or comply with law.

Meta Pixel and cookie choices

If you allow measurement, the Meta Pixel records page views and website actions such as starting a request, calling, emailing or opening WhatsApp. Meta may use cookies or similar technology to provide measurement, advertising delivery and audience services. The Pixel remains off when you decline.

Your choice is stored in this browser so the website can remember it. You can change that choice below. You may also review advertising choices through Meta Ad Preferences and Your Online Choices.

Retention and security

Information is kept only for as long as reasonably needed for enquiry handling, transport operations, business records, dispute management and legal obligations. Reasonable technical and organisational safeguards are used, but no internet transmission can be guaranteed completely secure.

Your rights

Depending on the law that applies to you, you may ask for access, correction, deletion, restriction or objection, or withdraw consent for optional processing. Contact us using the details above. You may also raise a concern with the South African Information Regulator or another competent data protection authority.
