How a transport job works

1

Transport request

You contact us with the collection point, destination, deceased details and urgency.

2

Documentation check

We confirm available documentation and any special handling requirements.

3

Route confirmation

We confirm distance, timing, driver, vehicle availability and estimated cost.

4

Collection

Our driver collects from the authorised facility or responsible party and records collection details.

5

Transport

Human remains are transported respectfully in the refrigerated vehicle.

6

Delivery & sign-off

The receiving party signs the receiving note and confirms handover.

7

Cleaning log

The vehicle compartment is cleaned and disinfected after transport.
