Human remains transport services

1

Local Collection & Transfer

For hospitals, mortuaries and funeral parlours requiring local movement of human remains.

2

Long-Distance Transport

Province-to-province transport support across South Africa.

3

Funeral Parlour Overflow Support

For funeral homes that do not have a suitable vehicle available or need additional transport capacity.

4

Repatriation Support

Transport support for inter-provincial or cross-border processes where authorised parties provide required documentation and permits.

5

After-Hours & Urgent Collections

Subject to availability and documentation.

6

Chain-of-Custody Job Packs

Structured transport documentation to support professional handling, collection, delivery and cleaning records.
